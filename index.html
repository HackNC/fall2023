What is HackNC?

HackNC is an annual, student-run hackathon hosted by the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Every fall, hundreds of students arrive at UNC to spend the weekend inventing something amazing. We’re one of the largest hackathons in the southeastern United States.
